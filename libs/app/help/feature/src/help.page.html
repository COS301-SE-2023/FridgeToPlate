Recommendation Page

There are two ways to navigate to this page:

  • Click on the "Recommendation" button on the navigation bar.
  • Click on the "Try something new" button on the home page.

The recommendation page is a page that is used to recommend recipes to the user based on the ingredients that they have in their "fridge".

How to Use the Recommendation Page

  • Navigate to the recommendation page by using one of the methods provided.
  • Enter ingredients from a drop-down selection.
  • Click on the "+" button to the right to add the ingredient to the list of ingredients.
  • Alternatively, scan the barcode of the ingredient and click on the displayed ingredient to add it.
  • Click on the trash can icon on the right of the added ingredient to remove it from the list.
  • To get specific recipe recommendations, apply filters to refine your preferences.
  • Click on the "Done" button to generate the recommendations.
  • It is a two-step process. Recipe suggestions will then be displayed.
